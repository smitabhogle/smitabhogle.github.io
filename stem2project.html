Designing a Mechanized Dog Feeder for Wheelchair Users

The objective of this project was to design and create an mechanized dog feeder for a client that uses a wheelchair. The dog feeder mechanism was modeled after an open source design. The wooden frame and food storage container were built according to the clients' specifications. Attached to the right is our final project presentation.
