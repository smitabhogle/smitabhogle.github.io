    STEM II- Assistive Technology

    At Mass Academy, the STEM / STW classes are taught by Dr. Crowthers. After STEM I was concluded in C Term, we began working on our STEM II Projects. We had the opportunity to choose our own groups, and work with anyone in our grade. Most groups design and manufacture an assistive device for a specific client. My group's project was titled "Designing a Mechanized Dog Feeder for Wheelchair Users".
